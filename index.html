Introduction

Firearms are powerful tools used for hunting, sport shooting, and defense. Understanding how they work and their history is key to responsible ownership.

Types of Firearms

  • Pistols: Small, handheld guns designed for personal defense.
  • Rifles: Long guns with higher accuracy over distances.
  • Shotguns: Designed for shooting a spread of pellets, ideal for close range.

Safety Tips

Firearm safety is crucial. Always assume a gun is loaded. Keep your finger off the trigger until ready to shoot. Store guns unloaded in a secure location.

Firearm Laws

Laws vary by country and state. Always research local regulations before purchasing or carrying a firearm. Licenses and background checks may be required.
